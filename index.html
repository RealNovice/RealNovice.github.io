Hello.

This is my personal website. Not because I needed one — but because at some point it felt important to leave a small, deliberate mark on the internet.

The early years

I grew up curious about how things worked. Screens, systems, people — all of it felt like a puzzle that never fully explained itself.

I learned by pulling things apart. Sometimes ideas. Sometimes expectations. Occasionally software.

Somewhere in the middle

Over time, ambition softened into taste. Speed turned into intention. I stopped trying to build everything and started caring about building the *right* things.

This phase was less about outcomes and more about learning what to ignore.

Now

Now I value clarity. Quiet confidence. Systems that disappear once they work.

This site is intentionally simple. No feeds. No metrics. Just a small window into how I think at this particular moment in time.
